Non-Euclidean Reversi (Othello)

  • Begin the game by placing 4 pieces (2 black, 2 white).
  • Then black and white take turns placing pieces that turn over lines of enemy peices
  • Lines may turn by any angle less than 45°
  • Due to combinatorics, the maximum line length is limited around 18 pieces
  • Please wait 5 seconds for the server to calculate lines
